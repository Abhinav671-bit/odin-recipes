Rasgulla

Description

This popular Bengali dessert is beloved across the South Asian diaspora! Soft round dumplings made from chenna (an Indian cottage cheese) and semolina flour are simmered in a cardamom and rosewater scented sugar syrup for a tasty sweet that is simply lovely.

While the origin story of this treat is hotly contested in some circles, I know rasgulla to be a famous Bengali sweet made from milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 litre whole milk
  • 2-3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 litre water
  • 1 teaspoon sooji
